# CardioVision: AI-Powered Coronary Artery Disease Analytics & Prediction System

## Project Overview

CardioVision is an end-to-end healthcare analytics and machine learning project designed to analyze, predict, and visualize Coronary Artery Disease (CAD) risk using clinical patient data.

## Features

* Data Cleaning & Preprocessing
* Exploratory Data Analysis (EDA)
* Risk Probability Prediction
* Risk Category Classification
* Interactive Plotly Dashboards
* Clinical Insights & Visualizations
* Executive PDF Report Generation

## Technologies Used

* Python
* Pandas
* NumPy
* Matplotlib
* Plotly
* Scikit-Learn

## Dataset

Heart Disease Dataset containing patient demographic and clinical attributes.

## Results

The project predicts heart disease risk probabilities and categorizes patients into Low, Medium, and High Risk groups, supporting early diagnosis and healthcare decision-making.
